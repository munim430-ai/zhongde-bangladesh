About ZhongDe

30+ Years of
Industrial Boiler Excellence

HeNan ZhongDe Boiler Equipment Co., Ltd. is a leading Chinese manufacturer of industrial boilers with over three decades of engineering experience. Headquartered in Henan Province, China, ZhongDe specializes in the design, manufacturing, and installation of high-efficiency industrial boilers for power generation, heating, and industrial processes.

As the official Bangladesh representative, we bring ZhongDe's world-class technology directly to Bangladeshi factories, textile mills, food processing plants, and power stations — with local support, commissioning, and after-sales service.

CFB Boiler

Circulating Fluidized Bed boilers ideal for burning low-quality coal, biomass, and industrial waste. Superior combustion efficiency with minimal emissions.

  • Capacity: 10–280 t/h
  • Pressure: 1.25–13.7 MPa
  • Temperature: Saturated / 540°C
  • Fuel: Coal, Biomass, Waste

  • Capacity: 2–130 t/h

Coal Boiler

Chain grate and stoker coal-fired boilers for reliable power generation and industrial steam supply. High thermal efficiency, robust construction.

  • Capacity: 2–75 t/h
  • Pressure: 1.0–5.29 MPa

  • Fuel: Bituminous, Anthracite

Oil & Gas Boiler

High-efficiency fire-tube and water-tube boilers for natural gas, diesel, and heavy fuel oil. Compact design, fully automatic operation.

  • Capacity: 1–20 t/h
  • Pressure: 0.7–2.5 MPa
